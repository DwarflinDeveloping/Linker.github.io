About Linker

Linker is an open source Discord bot that automatically generates links for you.

You want to link a website in a conversation?
Concentrate on the important things and let Linker do the work.

How does it work?

If you want Linker to generate a link to an article in a MediaWiki such as Wikipedia or Fandom, you only have to specify your Wiki and you are ready to go. By bracketing a word with { and }, for example {Earth}, the bot automatically generates the link and sends it directly below your message.

You want to give someone a summary on a topic? No problem.
Just put a $ in front of your article.
